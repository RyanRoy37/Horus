# Horus 
(Named after the Egyptian falcon-headed God)

A Mobile Application used to identify birds from their pictures and their sounds. The population of birds is restricted to, in and around the city of Bengaluru, India.

Think of Horus as a sort of Pokedex, but for birds.

Bengaluru boasts a diverse birdlife, with various species found throughout the city and its outskirts. Common birds include the House Crow, Jungle Crow, Black Kite, Common Myna, Jungle Myna, and House Sparrow. Notable locations for birdwatching include Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Bannerghatta National Park, and Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary. 


Due to limited acces to resources (both computing and storage), this application was built exclusively for the bird wacthers of Bengaluru only.

Horus makes use of 2 Machine Learning/Deep Learning models; One to identify the birds from a picture and the other from their sound.

Tech Stack
1. For the mobile app, we use Flutter(Dart)
2. To train the model we use Python(Tensorflow, scikit-learn, librosa, opencv ,etc.)
