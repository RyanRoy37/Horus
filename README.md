# Horus 
(Named after the Egyptian falcon-headed God)

A Mobile Application used to identify birds from their pictures and their sounds. The population of birds is restricted to, in and around the city of Bengaluru, India.

Think of Horus as a sort of Pokedex, but for birds.

Bengaluru boasts a diverse birdlife, with various species found throughout the city and its outskirts. Common birds include the House Crow, Jungle Crow, Black Kite, Common Myna, Jungle Myna, and House Sparrow. You can also find Indian Peafowl, Alexandrine Parakeet, and Black-crowned Night Herons, among others. Notable locations for birdwatching include Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Bannerghatta National Park, and Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary.
